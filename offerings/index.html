Private Sessions

I am available to teach private sessions in the convenience of the student’s home. Each session will be tailored to the needs of the individual student and all props and equipment will be provided. This option is excellent for you if:

  • You are new to yoga or would like to deepen your current practice
  • You are too busy to fit group yoga classes into your weekly schedule
  • You are interested in learning more about meditation and pranayama (breathing techniques) for stress reduction
  • You thrive under one-on-one instruction and seek individual guidance
  • You have specific questions and/or goals regarding your practice

Pricing:

$75 per session, which typically lasts one hour and fifteen minutes

Private sessions can include up to 2 people - a great option is to share the class with a friend or spouse and split the cost!

What to expect:

I offer a free and in-person consultation prior to your first booking. During this consultation, my goals are to understand what you would like to gain from your yoga practice, to obtain any information regarding your health and physical limitations (if any), and to answer any questions you may have. From there, I can begin to tailor classes around your intentions, goals, and physical needs so that you will get the most out of your time with me.

Although every session will vary with the students’ needs and goals, here is a general idea of what to expect:

  • Initial Centering
  • Pranayama (Breathwork)
  • Warm Up
  • Asana (Physical Yoga Postures)
  • Cool Down
  • Savasana (Final Resting Pose)
  • Ending Meditation (Optional)

If you have any questions or would like to book for your free consultation, please contact me.

Corporate Classes

Corporate wellness is an ever-growing trend these days and rightfully so! Corporations are beginning to realize the many benefits of yoga and offering weekly yoga classes to their employees as part of their benefits package and corporate wellness initiatives.

For those whose work is fairly sedentary and / or computer based, the many physical benefits of yoga include:

  • Reducing / relieving tension in the neck, shoulders, and hips from sitting for long periods of time
  • Building core strength to help reduce back pain
  • Building strength and flexibility in the body

For those who suffer from everyday stress or workplace anxiety, the mental benefits of yoga include:

  • Breathing techniques to calm and control the nervous system and the reduce stress responses in the body
  • Guided meditation to relax the body and calm the mind

In general, students who begin a regular yoga practice can expect:

  • An increased sense of well-being
  • A beginning awareness and control of the mind/ego
  • Greater focus, mental clarity, and creativity

Class options:

Lunchtime Wellness Break - a 1 hour class from 12:00 pm- 1:00pm*, designed to ward off the mid-day slump by refreshing the body and rejuvenating the mind

Postwork Wind Down - a 1 hour class from 5:30pm-6:30pm*, designed to relieve workday stress and frustrations by stretching the body, clearing the mind, and letting go of the day

*Class times may vary depending on the day and needs of the client
